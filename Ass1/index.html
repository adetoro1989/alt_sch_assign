Name: Olanrewaju Adetunji Adetoro

Occupation: IT Professional

Adetunji Olanrewaju Adetoro is a seasoned tech specialist with a focus on managing IT service delivery in enterprise environments. With 10 years of experience in the information technology field, he has spent 3 years specializing in IT service management and delivery within the International Development sector.

Olanrewaju is also pursuing a Cloud Engineering diploma at AltSchool Africa’s School of Engineering. He enrolled in this program to expand his knowledge and skills in IT service delivery, particularly in cloud technologies. Naturally curious and a problem-solver, Olanrewaju is always exploring innovative approaches to his work. By the end of his one-year program, he aims to acquire the necessary skills to solve complex problems in cloud-based systems administration and improve service delivery for diverse end users across various sectors.
